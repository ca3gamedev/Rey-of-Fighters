=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Meifu no Mon - F or B, y

Soukatsu Satsu - F or B, b


-- Command Moves
Tachikaze - F, x


-- Super Moves
Yonokaze - B, DB, D, DF, F, x or a or y or b

Wanhyou Tokobuse - D, DB, B, x

Wanhyou Mametsu - D, DB, B, y

Hyouga - D, DB, B, a or b

Amatsukaze - F, D, DF, x or y

Shin Koto Tsuki: Souga - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, a or b


-- DM(Normally: Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode: No Cost)
Yami Doukoku - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y

Jinryu Yamabuki - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- SDM(Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Jinryu Yamabuki - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, ab
