EX Terry Bogard(KOF'02UM Style) by Ikaruga
for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Grasping Upper - F or B, y

Buster Throw - F or B, b


-- Command Moves
Combination - Close Hard Punch (when hit) -> F, y

Rising Upper - DF, y


-- Super Moves
Burn Knuckle - D, DB, B, x or y

Power Wave - D, DF, F, x or y

Crack Shoot - D, DB, B, a or b

Power Dunk  - F, D, DF, a or b
 ->Breaking - x+a(only Light Power Dunk)
 
Power Charge - B, DB, D, DF, F, a or b
 ->Follow Up - F, F, a or b(2 times)


-- DM(Normally: Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode: No Cost)
Power Geyser - D, DB, B, DB, F, x or y

Buster Wolf - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- SDM(Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Triple Geyser - D, DB, B, DB, F, xy


-- MAX2(In low life, Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Power Stream - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, xy
