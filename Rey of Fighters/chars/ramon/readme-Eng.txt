  Controls
  --------

    Basic Controls
    --------------
      D - down
      F - forward
      U - up
      B - back
      x - button X (weak punch)
      y - button Y (strong punch)
      z - button Z (armor mode)
      a - button A (weak kick)
      b - button B (strong kick)
      c - button C (counter mode)

    Standard Movement
    -----------------
      U              - Jump
      UF             - Jump forwards
      F              - Walk forwards
      DF             - Crouch
      D              - Crouch
      DB             - Crouch or low guard
      B              - Walk backwards or high guard
      UB             - Jump back
      F, F           - Forward dash
      B, B           - Back dash
      a+x            - Recovery (when falling)
       
    Basic Standing Attacks
    ----------------------
      x              - W. Punch
      y              - S. Punch
      a              - W. Kick
      b              - S. Kick
      b+y            - Knockdown attack
      F + a         - Low Air Drop Kick 

    Basic Crouching Attacks
    -----------------------
      x             - Crouch W. Punch
      y             - Crouch S. Punch
      a             - Crouch W. Kick
      b             - Crouch S. Kick
      FD + a        - Low Air Drop Kick

    Basic Air Attacks
    -----------------
      x             - Jump W. Punch
      y             - Jump S. Punch
      a             - Jump W. Kick
      b             - Jump S. Kick
      b+y           - Knockdown attack

    Throws
    ------
      F/B + y (when close) - Flying Maya
      F/B + b (when close) - Arm Whip

    Special Moves
    ---------------
      Rolling Sobbat - F, D, DF + a or b 

      ¦¦Flying Body Attack - (Until Rolling Sobbat) F, D, DF + a or b 

        ¦¦Pulling Up Attack - (Until Flying Body Attack)D, D + x or y (SuperCancel enable)

      Tiger Neck Chain - (Near Opponent) B, DB, D, DF, F + x or y 

      Somersault - B, DB, D, DF, F + a or b 

      Feint Step - D, DB, B + x or y

      Tiger Neck Screw - F, D, DF + x or y

      Tiger Road - B, D, DB + a or b

      Tiger Hip - (Until Tiger Road) a or x or y (SuperCancel enable)

      Cross Chop - (when ramon touch corner while doing Tiger Road) b
              

    Hyper Moves
    -------------
      Tiger Spin - (Near Opponent) F, DF, D, DB, B X 2 + x or y 

      El.Diablo.Amarillo.Ramon - D, DB, B, DB, D, DF, F + a or b  

      Tiger Spin(Max) - (Near Opponent)  F, DF, D, DB, B X 2 + xy or z
