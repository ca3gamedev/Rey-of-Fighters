Iori Yagami(KOF'02UM Style) by Ikaruga
for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Saka Hagi - F, y

Saka Saka Hagi - F, b


-- Command Moves
Ge Shiki: Yume Biki - F, x -> x

Ge Shiki: Goufu In "Shini Gami" - F, a

Ge Shiki: Yuri Ori - (in air)B, a


-- Super Moves
108 Shiki: Yami Barai - D, DF, F, x or y

100 Shiki: Oni Yaki - F, D, DF, x or y

127 Shiki: Aoi Hana - D, DB, B, x or y(3 times)

212 Shiki: Kototsuki In - F, DF, D, DB, B, a or b

Kuzu Kaze - F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y


-- DM(Normally: Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode: No Cost)
Kin 1211 Shiki: Ya Otome - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y

Ura 311 Shiki: Saku Tsuma Gushi - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- SDM(Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Kin 1211 Shiki: Ya Otome - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, xy


-- MAX2(In low life, Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Ura 1029 Shiki: Homura Hotogi - D, DB, B, DB, D, DF, F, x+y
