Benimaru Nikaido(KOF'98 Style) by Ikaruga
for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

*Open Source

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode(ADV)/Power Charge(EX)
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Run(ADV)/Front Step(EX) - F, F
Back Step - B, B
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll(ADV)/Evade(EX) - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Maximum Mode(ADV) - x+a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Power Charge(EX) - x+a+y
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling


-- Throw
Catch and Shoot - F or B, y

Front Suplex - F or B, b

Spinning Knee Drop - F or B or U, Y or B


-- Command Moves
Jack Knife Kick - F, a

Flying Drill - U, b


-- Super Moves
Raijin Ken - D, DF, F, x or y(can use in air)

Shinku Katate Goma - D, DB, B, x or y

Iai Geri - D, DF, F, a or b

Super Inazuma Kick - F, D, DF, a or b

Handou Sandan Geri - F, DF, D, DB, B, a or b

Benimaru Collider - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, x or y


-- DM(ADV: Requires 1 Power Bars / EX: MAX Mode or In low life)
Raikou Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Elec-Trigger - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y


-- SDM(ADV: In MAX Mode & Requires 1 Power Bars / EX: In MAX Mode & In low life)
Raikou Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Elec-Trigger - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y
