Introduction to Yamazaki





Thanks for trying out Ryuji Yamazaki from The King Of Fighters 2002. This game was developed by SNK Playmore & Eolith. What else is needed to say ? Yamazaki appeared in Fatal Fury 3 (Garou Densetsu 3 in Japan) as the main boss of the game, he then appeared as a regular character in every Fatal Fury Real Bout series. His KOF appearance started from '97 (he was hired by Geese to help Billy investigate on the Orochi power) with a new outfit compared to Fatal Fury. He then came back in '98, 2002 and 2003 (where he came back to his old school outfit).


Character Appearances : Fatal Fury 3 - FF : Real Bout Series (1,Special,2) - FF : Wild Ambition - KOF'97, '98, 2000 (striker), 2002 & 2003.
Cameo Appearances : KOF95 - NeoGeoLand (Japan).
Voice Actor : Koji Ishii
Also the voice actor of : Sokaku (Fatal Fury 3+)
Official Music Themes : C62 - Shirokuni (FF3 - KOF'97 & '98) - Yuuwaku No Toki (KOF '02) - Black Rose (Fatal Fury : Wild Ambition)
Home-made Music Theme : KOF'97 Team Theme (Beat92Box - SNK Soundworks) - Click here to get it.
