=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Tani Otoshi - F, y

Tomoe Nage - F, b


-- Command Moves
Hyouchu Wari - F, x

Joudan Uke - F, a

Gedan Uke - DF, a


-- Super Moves
Ko'ou Ken - D, DF, F, x or y

Kohou - F, D, DF, x or y

Hien Shippu Kyaku(1) - F, DF, D, DB, B, a or b

Hien Shippu Kyaku(2) - DB, F, a or b

Zanretsu Ken - F, B, F, x or y

Mouko Raijin Setsu - D, DF, F, a or b

Kohou Sippu Ken - F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y


-- DM(Normally:Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode:No Cost)
Haoh Shoukou Ken - F, B, DB, D, DF, F, x or y

Ryuko Ranbu - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y


-- SDM(Normally:Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode:Requires 1 Power Bars)
Tenchi Haoh Ken - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x+y


-- MAX2(In low life, Normally:Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode:Requires 1 Power Bars)
Tenchi Jouge no Kamae - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, a+b
 -> Buriki Ranbu      - F, UF, F, F, DF, UF, UF, F
