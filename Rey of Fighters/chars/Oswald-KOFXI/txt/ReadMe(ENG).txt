Oswald(KOFXI Style) by Ikaruga
for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

*Original : Ahuron's Oswald + fix patch by me
*Open Source

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - None
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Skill Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Super Cancel - Super Moves -> DM(Requires 1 Skill Bar)
Dream Cancel - DM -> Leader DM(Requires 1 Skill Bar)


-- Throw
2 - F, y or b


-- Command Moves
10 - DF, x

J - F, x


-- Super Moves
Spade        - D, DB, B, x or y
 ->Follow Up - D, DB, B, x or y

Diamond      - D, DF, F, x or y
 ->Follow Up - D, DF, F, x or y

Club - D, DB, B, a or b

Heart - D, DF, F, a or b
 ->A  - D, DF, F, z

Q - F, D, DF, x or a or y or b

K - B, D, DB, a or b


-- DM(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Flush - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y

Royal Straight - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y


-- Leader DM(Requires 2 Power Bars)
JOKER - (charge)B, F, B, F, z
