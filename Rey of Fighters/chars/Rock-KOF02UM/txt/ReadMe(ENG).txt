=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Roll - x+a while attack hitting(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling
Maximum Mode - a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Quick Max - a+y while attack hitting(Requires 2 Power Bars)


-- Throw
Brain Shake - F, y

Koku Sen - F, b


-- Command Moves
Elbow Spike - F, x

Parabola Kick - F, a

Combination - close heavy kick (when hit) -> F, b


-- Super Moves
Reppu Ken - D, DF, F, x

Double Reppu Ken - D, DF, F, y

Rising Tuckle - (charge)D, U, x or y

Hard Edge - D, DB, B, x or y

Rage Run: type "Dunk" - D, DB, B, a

Rage Run: type "Shift" - D, DB, B, b

Crack Counter - D, DF, F, a or b

Shinku Nage  - 360, x or y
 -> Braking  - x+a
  -> Rasetsu - hold x+a after Braking


-- DM(Normally: Requires 1 Power Bars/MAX Mode: No Cost)
Raging Storm - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x or y

Shine Knuckle - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a or b


-- SDM(Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Raging Storm - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, x+y

Shine Knuckle - D, DF, F, D, DF, F, a+b


-- MAX2(In low life, Normally: Requires 3 Power Bars/MAX Mode: Requires 1 Power Bars)
Deadly Rave Neo - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, x
 -> Follow Up   - x, x, a, a, y, y, b, b -> D, DB, B, y
