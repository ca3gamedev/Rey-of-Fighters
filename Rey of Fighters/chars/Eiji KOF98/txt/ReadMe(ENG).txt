for WinMugen / Mugen 1.0

*Open Source

=====<Movelist>=====

-- Button Assign
x - Light Punch
y - Hard Punch
z - Knockdown Attack
a - Light Kick
b - Hard Kick
c - Maximum Mode(ADV)/Power Charge(EX)
start - Taunt


-- Moves
Run(ADV)/Front Step(EX) - F, F
Back Step - B, B
Knockdown Attack - y+b or z
GC Knockdown Attack - y+b or z while guarding
Roll(ADV)/Evade(EX) - x+a
GC Roll - x+a while guarding(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Maximum Mode(ADV) - x+a+y(Requires 1 Power Bar)
Power Charge(EX) - x+a+y
Safe Fall Recovery - x+a when nearing ground while falling


-- Throw
Kisaragi Ryu Renken Kyaku - F, y

Kuutyu Ippon Zeoi - F, b


-- Super Moves
Kikou Hou - D, DF, F, x or y

Kasumi Giri - D, DB, B, x or y

Ryuuei Jin - F, D, DF, x or y

Koppa Giri - D, DB, B, a or b

Tenma Kyaku - B, D, DB, a or b

Kage Utsushi - D, DF, F, a or b

Mukuro Nui - (in air)D, DF, F, x or y

-- DM(ADV: Requires 1 Power Bars / EX: MAX Mode or In low life)
Zantetsu Ha - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y

Zantetsu Tourou Ken - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, a or b


-- SDM(ADV: In MAX Mode & Requires 1 Power Bars / EX: In MAX Mode & In low life)
Zantetsu Ha - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y

Zantetsu Tourou Ken - D, DF, F, DF, D, DB, B, a or b

Yami Gari - F, DF, D, DB, B, F, DF, D, DB, B, x or y
